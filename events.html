Feed the Animals

Get a chance to feed the animals! All throughout August, come along anytime between 12-3pm to personally feed some of our more docile animals. Be sure to bring the family - children under 5 are free admission.

See them Up Close

Experience our exotics up close! Come September, we're opening up the chance to experience our more exotic animals up close for the first time. Take a trip with the wild cats in a protective cage, see eye-to-eye with our sharks, and roll around in the dinosaur enclosure in a specially designed sphere. Ages 15 and up, terms and conditions apply.
